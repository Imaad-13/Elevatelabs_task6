Password Strength Report

Passwords Tested:
1. abc123         - Simple (lowercase + numbers)     - Crack Time: <1 second
2. Abc123         - Medium (upper + lower + numbers) - Crack Time: Few seconds
3. Abc123!        - Strong (with symbol)             - Crack Time: Minutes
4. A$7kB!9pL@x    - Very Strong, 12+ chars           - Crack Time: Centuries
5. P@ssw0rd123456 - Common pattern, long             - Crack Time: Hours

Best Practices:
- Use a mix of upper/lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
- Make passwords at least 12 characters long.
- Avoid dictionary words and common phrases.
- Never reuse passwords across different platforms.

Common Password Attacks:
- Brute Force
- Dictionary Attacks
- Credential Stuffing
- Phishing

Summary:
Stronger passwords greatly improve resistance to automated attacks. Length and randomness are crucial.
